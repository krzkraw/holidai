### O obiekcie (Opis)

Comfortable Accommodations: Hotel Venera in Sarandë offers comfortable rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, and city views. Each room includes a balcony, coffee machine, and TV.

Essential Facilities: Guests enjoy free WiFi, daily housekeeping service, and bicycle parking. Additional amenities include a work desk, refrigerator, and wardrobe.

Convenient Location: Saranda City Beach is a 6-minute walk away. Reception staff speak English and Italian.
