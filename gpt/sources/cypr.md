### O obiekcie (Opis)

Set on a secluded bay, Queens Bay Hotel features outdoor pools, a padle court and a fitness room with sauna. Its extensive gardens stretch down to the beach. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

All guest rooms at Queens Bay Hotel are air-conditioned and equipped with satellite TV, hairdryer and a private bathroom.

Queens Bay features a pool terrace offering panoramic sea and sunset views. Guests can also relax in the sauna or try their skills in French Bowls, table tennis and archery.

The Cassiopia Restaurant offers a variety of fresh Mediterranean dishes. In the evening, guests can have a drink and enjoy live music entertainment at the hotel’s bar.

Hotel Queens Bay is 0.9 mi away from Kissonerga Village and 10 minutes’ driving from Paphos Town. The hotel is half way between the ancient site of The Tombs of the Kings and the popular Coral Bay. Free on-site parking is possible.

### O obiekcie (Opis)

Exclusive accommodations is on offer at Paphos' Capital Coast Resort and Spa. This beachfront resort has a Spa, featuring a hot tub, hammam, sauna and massage treatments.

The bright guest rooms have contemporary and elegant décor. Some include a fully equipped kitchen with oven and dishwasher. There is a large living area fitted with flat-screen satellite TV and DVD player.

Capital Coast Resort and Spa’s restaurant serves a combination of traditional and international dishes. The hotel offers an outdoor pool bar and a roof top bar with views over the Mediterranean Sea. Certified Cypriot breakfast is served in the morning.

With 2 pools, a gym with cardio-fitness activities, and a tennis court, the resort provides an array of in-house activities. Diving and snorkeling services are available at the nearby beach.

For guests staying at least 3 nights, the hotel offers free 2-way transfer from Paphos International Airport. From/to Larnaca Airport, transfers can be arranged at an additional cost.

Guests are kindly requested to sent their flight details at least 2 days in advance, in order for the hotel to arrange the pick-up.Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Just 2133 feet from the Capital Coast, the Tombs of the Kings are within walking distance. Capital Coast Resort & Spa is located within a short driving distance from the 4 local Golf Courses.
