- **Opis obiektu:**
Gloria Maris Hotel Suites and Villas is a charming adult only resort located between Aghios Sostis and Keri Lake in Porto Koukla.The resort offers well-decorated, well-equipped rooms with panoramic sea views. Laganas center is 3 km from the Hotel.

All of the rooms at Gloria Maris are comfortable with air conditioning and deluxe ensuite bathrooms. Each room also includes a private balcony.

Gloria Maris Hotel has a impressive outdoor swimming pool surrounded by tropical trees. Guests can relax in a lounger on the sun terrace or sip cocktails from Gloria Maris’s pool bar.

Breakfast is available, served buffet style in the Votsala Restaurant.

Gloria Maris is less than 12 km from the Dionysios Solomos Airport.  Free parking is available on-site.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 8,7.
