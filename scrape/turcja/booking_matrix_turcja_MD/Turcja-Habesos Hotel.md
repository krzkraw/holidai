- **Opis obiektu:**
Situated in Kas, recently renovated Habesos Hotel overlooks the Mediterranean Sea and Taurus Mountains. It has a seasonal outdoor pool and an al fresco restaurant. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel.

Private balconies and minibars are featured in all Habesos rooms. Each is air conditioned and has satellite TV channels. Individual bathrooms include hairdryers.

Guests can lounge by the pool, enjoying a drink from the poolside bar. Hotel Habesos also offers a spa bath and a bike rental service. To arrange their time out, visitors are welcome to consult the tour desk.

The hotel has a choice of 2 restaurants, indoors and outdoors. Buffet breakfast is served each morning and can be also enjoyed on the terrace overlooking the sea and the mountains.

Habesos Hotel is located 160 km from Dalaman Airport and 185 km from Antalya.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 9,5.
