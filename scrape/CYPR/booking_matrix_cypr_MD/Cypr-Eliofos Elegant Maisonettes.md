- **Opis obiektu:**
Quietly located at Polis Chrysochous and surrounded by a well-tended garden, Eliofos Elegant Maisonettes offers air-conditioned maisonettes with a seating area. Akamas Peninsula is 15 km away and Latchi Village is a 5-minute drive.

All units at Eliofos include a well-equipped kitchenette with fridge, kitchenware and electric kettle. Every Maisonette features a  flat-screen smart TV and offers garden views. The private bathroom comes with a shower.

At a 3-minute walk, guests can find restaurants, bars and supermarkets. The Baths of Aphrodite are 5 km away. The municipal beach of Latchi lies within 150 metres and the camping is a 2-minute drive. Paphos International Airport is at a distance of 41 km. Free private parking is available on site.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 9,5.
