- **Opis obiektu:**
Constantinou Bros Athena Royal Beach Hotel is situated on Kato Paphos waterfront, just 4 km from the lively town centre. It boasts 3 swimming pools, 2 hot tubs and a tennis court. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Athena Royal offers large, well-appointed rooms with twin or double beds. All rooms have spacious balconies and satellite TV. Some feature views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Hotel's restaurant offers attentive service and traditional Cypriot cuisine. The expansive terrace offers outside dining, with panoramic views.

Constantinou Bros Athena Royal Beach Hotel provides numerous leisure services and activities. For indulgence, it offers a sauna, steam bath and wellness centre where guests can enjoy massages and beauty treatments. Two exclusive golf-courses are also within 3 km of the hotel.

Air conditioning (May-Oct) and Central heating (Nov-Apr).

Hotel is located 15 km away from Paphos Airport and 140 km from Larnaca Airport. Free private parking is available.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 8,9.
