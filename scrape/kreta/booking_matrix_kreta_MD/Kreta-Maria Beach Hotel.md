- **Opis obiektu:**
Situated on a sandy organised beach, Maria Beach Hotel is 600 metres from the centre of Kissamos Town. It boasts a traditional, beachfront restaurant with an unlimited sea-view terrace and self-catering accommodation with free WiFi and a balcony or patio.

The spacious studios and apartments at Maria Beach offer a kitchenette with cooking hobs, fridge and small dining area. Air conditioning, a TV and a safety deposit box are standard. Some offer Cretan Sea views.

Buffet breakfast is prepared each morning and can be enjoyed on the beach front terrace, offering panoramic views of Kissamos Bay. The modernly decorated restaurant and bar serves traditional Cretan cuisine and refreshing drinks.

Sunbeds and umbrellas are provided at the beach area with extra charge

Maria Beach Hotel is 15 km from Balos Beach, whereas Falassarna Beach is 13 km away. Chania City is at a distance of 38 km. Transfers from/to Chania Airport and Heraklion Airport are available upon request with surcharge.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 9,5.
