- **Opis obiektu:**
Located right on the Rethymno's beach promenade, only 10 meters away from the beach, Batis Aero Beachfront Wellbeing Hotel offers accommodation with sea view from most of its rooms. Facilities include a beach bar, beach sun beds upon charge , a rooftop terrace and a swimming pool. 

All rooms and suites have a furnished balcony, some with views of the sea. The rooms, include a satellite TV, Free WiFi and hairdryer available. Safety box is free of charge.

The hotel's beach bar serves an all day menu with refreshing cocktails and beverages, offering beach service and room service.  Water sports are available on the beach where the lifeguard ensures guests’ safety.

Batis Aero Beachfront Wellbeing Hotel’s offers a revamped swimming pool. Guests have free access to the hotel’s library, Wi-Fi hotspot and a 24h gym. Guests have free access to the hotel’s library and Wi-Fi hotspot.

Hotel Batis is 800 metres from the lively centre of Rethymnon. The port is 1 km away, and Chania International Airport is 75 km away.

Parom bardzo się podoba ta lokalizacja – za pobyt dla 2 osób oceniają ją na 9,5.
